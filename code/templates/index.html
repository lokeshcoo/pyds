What is the Stock Market? The stock market, also known as the equity market, is a public marketplace where shares of publicly traded companies are bought and sold. It serves as a platform for companies to raise capital by issuing stocks and for investors to buy and sell those stocks. The stock market plays a crucial role in the economy by facilitating the flow of funds between investors and companies.
